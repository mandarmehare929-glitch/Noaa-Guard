NOAA-GUARD : SATELLITE DEFENSE INITIATIVE

The Mission

As we approach the Solar Maximum 2025-26, the frequency of X-class solar flares is increasing. NOAA-GUARD acts as an autonomous watchdog to prevent a global communication blackout.

Space Assets

  • DSCOVR Satellite: Provides early warning for solar wind speed and plasma density from the L1 Lagrange point.
  • SDO (NASA): High-resolution imagery of the solar corona using AIA 171 & 193 filters.

Autonomous Defense Protocols

When wind speeds exceed safety thresholds (e.g., > 600 km/s), the system triggers "GHOST MODE". This logic is based on the Faraday Cage Principle—protecting sensitive electronics from electromagnetic induction during a geomagnetic storm.
